# {metadata.title}

In this recipe, you'll learn about the general approach to integrating an ERP system with Medusa.

Businesses often rely on an ERP system to manage important and custom aspects of their use case. They use an ERP system to centralize their data, such as special product pricing; manage custom business rules that change how their customers make purchases; and implement workflows around order processing and fulfillment.

When integrating the ERP system to other ecommerce platforms, you'll face complications as you try to customize existing flows in the platform to use data from the ERP system. For example, the ecommerce platform may not support purchasing products with custom pricing, or it may not allow you to restrict certain products to specific customers.
